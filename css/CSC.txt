Sub : ISSUE OF FREE Q&A BOOKS TO 10TH and +2 STUDENTS

CSC Profile:

Established in 1986, CSC has now emerged pioneer in Computer Education Training Institute in Tamil Nadu with specialisation in Computer Education for the past 36 years. CSC has a wide network of 402 centers in Tamil Nadu spreading over every nook and corner. Students in the remotest area can learn computer as we have centers in remotest places. Middle class people consider CSC as a boon to their advancement in Computer Education at affordable cost.

CSC Service to school students :

As a part of corporate social responsibility we are distributing free Q&A BOOK to students of 10th and +2 for the past 27 years on 14 titles in Tamil Nadu. We are spending Rs. 4 cr on this noble service.

We are giving this free copy of Q&A books to all 10th & +2 students all over Tamil Nadu only with a view to motivate them in getting good marks in their public exam. Every year substaintial number of students have been benefitted out of this Q&A BOOK by scoring high marks. We receive overwhelming response from parents / students / teachers for this book. With this inspiration to students and usefulness of this book, we are enthused to distribute 10,00,000 copies of Q&A BOOK for the current year.
